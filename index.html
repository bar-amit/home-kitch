About the restaurant

During the pandemic, many people remained trapped in foreign countries. Some have just been working abroad for a long time and are very homesick.

Perhaps you haven't eaten your mom's signature soup or your grandma's meat pie for many years, and you can't get the French meat from your aunt Zoey out of your head.

We are ready to help you prepare that very dish.

Our menu features the best homemade dishes of our customers from all over the world. Each dish has its own story and its own fans. Only dishes with a high rating are included in our menu.

To feel the taste of home, you can also create the same recipe with our chefs, they will take into account all the wishes and ingredients, contact your grandmother to clarify the subtleties of the recipe and together we will create the same dish from the past.

You and your friends will be able to order it via a special link, and if it becomes popular, we will be able to include it in the main menu and you will receive royalties from sales.
